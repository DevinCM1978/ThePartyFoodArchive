The Party Food Archive: A Digital Library of Retro Family Recipes

Welcome!



Welcome to The Party Food Archive: A Digital Library of Retro Family Recipes,
a repository of twenty appetizer-type food and drink recipes, all written by hand,
that I have found from scouring the kitchen shelves and pantries of relatives and friends.
Spanning the 1970s through the 1990s, these recipes are a true cultural "snapshot" of
my southwest Louisiana family's culinary history, as they represent treats that we made and enjoyed,
time and time again, for get-togethers, celebrations, and holidays.
Not only are they posted here to share with all of you, but in many cases,
they have been digitized so that they will be preserved - a few were in such aged condition
that they crumbled and shed bits onto my equipment as I scanned them!

To navigate this site, simply use the horizontal menu bar at the top of this screen; click or tap:

* All Recipes A-Z for an alphabetical index of all twenty recipes
